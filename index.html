Empowering Youth With Advanced Computational Resources to Promote Scientific Creativity

Epoch aims to give IMSA students access to a platform to accelerate training for machine learning (ML), computational chemistry, and physics, in addition to various topics and projects like SIR studies, Quantopian contests, Kaggle challenges, FRC programming, and AI in medicine.

  • Computation is the tool of:
  • Biology
  • Medicine
  • Physics
  • Astronomy
  • Translation
  • Finance
  • The Future

However, it is not widely available to high school students!
